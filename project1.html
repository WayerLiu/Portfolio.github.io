Empowering Moms, Elevating Mornings:

Redefining the Daily Commute

Jane, a 30-year-old mother with a 3-year-old child, faces a daily challenge with her child's Kindergarten located at Vigie and her workplace at EPFL, while residing in Bourdonette. Her daily commute in opposite directions consumes 20 precious minutes. Focusing on enhancing the morning drop-off routine, where she embarks on her journey with her son, is crucial. Improving this touchpoint can significantly save her time and energy, all while ensuring her child's well-being during the commute.

Our self-driving bubble cars optimize Jane's morning routine, seamlessly transitioning from daycare drop-off to her work commute. These cars adapt to her schedule, prioritizing child safety with advanced features, reducing stress and inefficiencies.

Another solution is a mobile childcare van, revolutionizing the drop-off process for parents like Jane. It efficiently picks up children from various locations, saving time and energy while ensuring safety and engagement during transit. The van transforms into an outdoor daycare for versatility.
