Nature Meets Nurture:

Revitalizing Spaces, Enriching Student Life

Vortex, a grand dormitory building in Lausanne, offers students spacious and charming accommodations along with stunning views. The building features inviting social spaces on both the first floor and the rooftop, enhancing the communal experience. However, we found that the central outdoor area, while expansive, appears somewhat barren. To enrich this space, introducing elements that evoke a sense of nature could transform it into a more vibrant and naturally appealing area, making it an even more enjoyable part of the student living experience.

We've transformed the courtyard into a striking blend of architecture and nature. Inspired by Frei Otto's design, a lightweight tensile tent structure now graces the area, adding a touch of elegance and innovation. This structure, coupled with the pastoral charm of grazing sheep, converts the previously barren space into a vibrant, nature-infused haven. It's a unique fusion that not only enhances the aesthetic appeal but also creates an environment for students to relax, socialize, and connect with nature amidst their busy academic lives.
