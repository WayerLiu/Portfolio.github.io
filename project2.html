Netting Change:

From Ocean Waste to Everyday Elegance

Amid growing concerns for ocean protection, our initiative repurposes discarded fishing nets into stylish backpacks. We prevent waste from harming marine life and create valuable, functional products, symbolizing environmental stewardship integrated into daily life.

We collect waste nets from aquaculture and fishing industries to create quality backpacks. Our website and marketing raise awareness of recycling fishing nets for ocean conservation. Customers receive a badge as a token of gratitude for contributing to ocean preservation.
